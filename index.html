Multimodal Deception Detection

We explore a new generation of computational tools for joint modeling of physiological and linguistic signals of human behavior. Our research work includes the construction of multimodal resources for the deception detection task, including novel physio-linguistic datasets as well as the development of multimodal approaches that integrate physiological features (e.g., heart rate, respiration rate, galvanic skin response, skin temperature), and linguistic features.

Projects

Deception Detection from Text

This project aims to identify and model linguistic patterns associated to deceptive and truthful text by extracting linguistic features such as, n-grams, shallow and deep syntax features, readability features, and semantic features. .

Multimodal Deception Detection

In this project we perform the deception detection task by integrating features from different data streams such as linguistic, visual, physiological, and thermal modalities. This project also targets the construction of multimodal resources for this task.

Real-Life Deception Detection

In this project we approach the identification of deceivers in real-life scenarios such as one-on-one interviews and court trials. We use video recordings taken in natural settings and apply multimodal approaches for deception detection, which have shown to provide valuable visual and linguistic patterns to discriminate between liars and truth tellers.

Open Domain Deception

A crowdsourced deception dataset consisting of short open domain truths and lies from 512 users. Seven lies and seven truths are provided for each user. The dataset also includes user's demographic information, such as gender, age, country of origin, and education level.(August 27, 2015)

Cross-Cultural Deception

A deception dataset covering four different cultures: US, India, Mexico, and Romania. Each dataset consists of short deceptive and truthful essays for three topics: opinions on abortion, opinions on death penalty, and feelings about a best friend. (November 20, 2014)

Real-life Deception

A multimodal dataset consisting of real-life deception: deceptive and truthful trial testimonies, manually transcribed and annotated. The dataset includes 121 short videos, along with their transcriptions and gesture annotations. (June 15, 2016)
